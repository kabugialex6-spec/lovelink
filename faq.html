Frequently Asked Questions

How does Love Link work?
Love Link connects people based on mutual interests. Create a profile, browse matches, swipe right to like someone, and if they like you back, it's a match! You can then start chatting.
Is Love Link free to use?
Yes! Love Link is completely free. You can create a profile, browse matches, and chat with your matches without any cost.
How do I delete my account?
Currently, you can clear your data by clearing your browser's localStorage. We're working on an account deletion feature for future updates.
Can I report inappropriate behavior?
Absolutely. Your safety is our priority. If someone makes you feel uncomfortable or violates our guidelines, please email us at report@lovelink.example.com with details.
How are matches determined?
Matches are based on the gender preference you select. When both users swipe right on each other's profiles, it creates a match allowing you to chat.
What happens if I unmatched someone?
If you unmatched someone, the chat will be archived. You won't see their profile again in your discovery feed unless you reset your preferences.

Still have questions? Contact us at support@lovelink.example.com

We typically respond within 24 hours.
