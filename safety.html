Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority

Protect Your Personal Information

Never share your home address, workplace details, financial information, or any sensitive personal data with someone you just met online.

Meet in Public Places

Always choose public, well-lit locations for first meetings. Coffee shops, restaurants, or parks are great options. Avoid private locations.

Tell Friends or Family

Let someone know where you're going, who you're meeting, and when you expect to return. Consider sharing your location with a trusted friend.

Arrange Your Own Transportation

Use your own transportation or ride-sharing services. Don't accept rides from someone you just met.

Stay Sober and Aware

Keep a clear mind during dates. Be cautious with alcohol consumption and never leave your drink unattended.

Keep Your Phone Charged

Make sure your phone is charged and with you at all times. Have emergency contacts easily accessible.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong or uncomfortable, trust your gut. You have the right to leave any situation that makes you feel unsafe.

If you're in immediate danger, call emergency services (911 in the US, 112 in EU).

Report Suspicious Behavior

If someone makes you feel uncomfortable or violates our community guidelines, please report them immediately through the app.

Love Link is committed to creating a safe dating environment for everyone.

For emergencies or immediate assistance, contact local authorities.
